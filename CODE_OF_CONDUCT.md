# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Trolling, insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and public interactions.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Maintainers will follow these guidelines in determining consequences:
- Correction
- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban
